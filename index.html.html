5
Choose How Hours Are Redistributed
Select a redistribution mode. The removed member's pending hours (Col V) get pushed to the remaining team's Col W.
Suggested (balanced)
Equalises New Total Alloc across the whole team. Best for fairness.
Manual selection
Click specific team members to absorb. Full control. Sorted by most room first.
Fill lowest first
Fills the least-loaded members up to team avg before moving to others. Waterfall style.
Everyone equally
Splits freed hours evenly across all remaining members regardless of current load.
6
Read the Status Badges
Each member gets a status badge based on their predicted occupancy after redistribution.
OK
Occupancy ≤100%. Member is within safe limits, not overloaded.
Absorbing
Taking on redistributed hours and still within safe occupancy.
Stretched
Occupancy 101–115%. Manageable but monitor closely.
Overloaded
Occupancy >115%. Removal of this member is not advisable.
7
Read the Absorber Panel
When a mode is selected (other than Everyone equally), the sidebar shows who absorbs what — sorted by most room first based on their New Total Alloc vs team average.
